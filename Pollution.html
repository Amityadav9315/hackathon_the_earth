Air Pollution

Emissions from vehicles, factories, and power plants fill the air with toxic substances. This causes smog, acid rain, and global warming, while increasing respiratory diseases in humans.

Water Pollution

Oceans, rivers, and lakes are being poisoned with sewage, plastic waste, and chemicals. This endangers aquatic life and contaminates drinking water for humans.

Plastic Waste

Single-use plastics take hundreds of years to decompose, polluting land and sea. Wildlife often mistake it for food, leading to fatal consequences.

Climate Change

Excessive greenhouse gases are heating up the planet. Glaciers are melting, sea levels are rising, and extreme weather is increasing.
